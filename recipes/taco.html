Taco Recipe

Taco Recipe from true mexico



Are you Ready for a taste of authentic mexican food


You will need:

  • 2 Chicken Breast
  • Soft Tortilla
  • 5 Spring Onion
  • 3 Fresh Tomato
  • Handful of Parsley
  • Cayanne Peper
  • 1 Chilli
  • Chilli Powder


Recipie

  1. Chop Spring Onion, Tomato and Parsley Fine and Put into a bowl. Season with a generous pinch of salt and peper, then mix cover and put in the fridge
  2. Chop Chicken into small pieces
  3. Put Chicken Into Frying Pan and add spices, chilli and some butter
  4. Fry Chicken till cooked and tender
  5. Heat Tortilla in Microwave
  6. Serve on Individual Plates
  7. Construct Taco using 3 ingrediants and enjoy
