Chilli

This recipie has been forged from the best food asgard has to offfer,
Thor says this is his favourite of the land


This recipie needs the following:

  • 500g Mince
  • 5 Spring Onions
  • 1 Red Chilli
  • 1 Can of Chopped Tomatoes
  • Sweetcorn
  • Peas
  • 200ml Water
  • 1 Pack of Chilli Mix
  • Olive Oil
  • Cayane Pepper


This is how the make the Chilli:

  1. Brown Mince in a frying pan
  2. Chop all da Veg
  3. Drain Mince and transfer to sauce pan
  4. Add Tomatoes to pan, mix water and chilli mix and add to pan
  5. bring up to heat, add veg 1 at a time and mix as added
  6. Add Chilli and Cayane Pepper to pan
  7. Simmer on a low heat and stir ocasionally for 20 mins
  8. Serve and enjoy
