The Curry

Now for the asgardian curry, a true cult classic



This was bought back from a trip to india on earth


Ingrediants required:


  • Chicken Breast
  • Jar of Curry Sauce
  • Spring Onion
  • Peas
  • Chilli
  • Microwave Rice

How to cook the curry:

  1. Finely cut chilli and spring onion, then dice the chicken breast
  2. Add Chicken Breast to frying pan and cook till tender
  3. Add Sping Onion, pes and chilli to pan, then add curry sauce and simmer for 20 mins
  4. Put Rice in Microwave to cook
  5. Add rice to plate
  6. Pour curry on top and enjoy
